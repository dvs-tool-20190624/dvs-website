Resources

Here is a list of resources that may help you with finances. The resources cover crowdfunding for survivors, locating shelters, financial education, and scholarships to pursue higher education.

YouHelp

YouHelp is a crowdfunding resource for survivors. You can fundraise directly for yourself through this organization, or contribute funds to others.

domesticshelters.org

This resource allows you to search for the nearest domestic violence shelter, and shows the services they provide. Shelters are great places to get housing assistance, referrals from professionals for services, and more. This can help ease the burden of your finances.

National Coalition Against Domestic Violence

The link will will take you directly to NCADV's financial education page. This resource provides financial education via a free webinar series and pdf book.

Women's Independent Scholarship Program

This program provides funding for domestic violence survivors wanting to pursue higher education.
