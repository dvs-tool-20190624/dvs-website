Survivor Expense Calculator

How It Works

The Survivor Expense Tool is designed to give you a look into your finances, so you can figure out your monthly expenditures, and break the cycle by regaining your financial security.

Using this tool, you'll be able to track and see what your monthly expeditures are, and use this information to plan for your future and relocation. Enter in your bills, expenses, and keep track of them right inside the calculator.

What's Needed

Before starting, take a moment to gather any and all bills, expenses, and payments you'll be responsible for.

Use this list to help you figure out what you'll need to keep track of.

Expenses

Needs

  • - Transportation (Public Transportation, Car Payment, Car Insurance, etc)
  • - Food & Nutrition
  • - Healthcare (Insurance, Medication, etc)
  • - Childcare
  • - Education (Tuition, supplies, etc)
  • - Debt

Relocation

  • - Hotel & Temporary Housing
  • - Moving Expenses (Rental fees, Utlity Fees, Moving truck/help, Storage, etc)
  • - Communication (Cell Phone, Change of Address)
  • - Transportation (Car Rental & Gas, Flight, Bus Ticket, etc)

Income Sources

  • - Social Benefits (SSI, SNAP, Cash Aid, etc)
  • - Charity & Donations
  • - Employment
